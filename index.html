About this Application

This Text Summarization tool uses two approaches that involve the Extractive and the Abstractive methods to generate a summary. The Extractive Approach uses the TFIDF library and the Abstractive Approach uses the GPT module based on Hugging face transformers to paraphrase the sentences and produce a short and a coherent summary.
